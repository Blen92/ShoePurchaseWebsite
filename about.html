About Us

SHOEMAKERS SINCE 1825

Our story started just about 200 years back when Cyrus and James made a shoe from sheepskin off-cuts. At the time it was notable; a mix of development and craftsmanship that is stayed at the core of what we do. Furthermore, while now, as usual, each match of our shoes starts with a last cut by hand from a solitary square of hornbeam, propelled development strategies, innovations and contemporary materials enable us to convey flawlessness customized for the cutting edge world.

From the earliest starting point we’ve generally thought in an unexpected way. What’s more, done things another way as well. From the shoe made by Cyrus and James in 1825 to the world’s first foot-molded shoe; from our children’s reaches in entire sizes, half sizes and a selection of widths to our best in class iPad foot check; from inventive underneath padding to quick prototyping and 3D printing, splendid thoughts are what separate us.
